NZ Benchmarks
🏠 HousingIdeally under 30% of income. Over 40% puts serious pressure on everything else.
🚗 TransportTarget under 15%. Factor in WOF, rego & maintenance — not just petrol.
🥦 Food & GroceriesNZ avg ~$150–$200/person/month. Meal planning makes a real difference.
💰 Savings RateExperts suggest 10–20% of income. Even 5% builds a solid habit over time.
📱 Bills & SubscriptionsAudit annually — most Kiwis overpay on power, broadband & streaming.
🎯 Debt RepaymentsKeep total debt repayments under 20% of income where possible.
